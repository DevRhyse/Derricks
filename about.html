About Us

Derrick's Electric & Design, your trusted partner for exceptional electric and electrical design solutions in California.

At Derrick's Electric & Design, we take pride in our ability to deliver top-notch electrical design solutions for a wide range of projects. With our expertise and dedication to excellence, we have become a trusted name in the industry. With years of experience, we have honed our skills in handling diverse electrical design projects, including residential, custom homes, restaurants, wineries, product wineries, and commercial spaces. Our team of skilled professionals is equipped to cater to your unique requirements and ensure outstanding results.
